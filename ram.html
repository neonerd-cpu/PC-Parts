RAM Buying Guide

How Much RAM Do You Need?

  • 16GB: Perfect for gaming and general use
  • 32GB: Ideal for gaming + streaming or content creation
  • 64GB+: Professional workstation for video editing, 3D rendering

DDR4 vs DDR5:

  • DDR4: More affordable, compatible with older systems
  • DDR5: Faster speeds, better for latest Intel/AMD platforms

Speed Recommendations:

  • Intel 12th-14th Gen: DDR5 5600-6400 MHz
  • AMD Ryzen 7000: DDR5 6000 MHz (sweet spot)
  • Budget Gaming: DDR4 3200-3600 MHz
