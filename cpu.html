CPU Buying Guide

What to Consider:

  • Core Count: More cores = better multitasking and productivity
  • Clock Speed: Higher GHz = faster single-threaded performance
  • Gaming: 6-8 cores are ideal for most games
  • Workstation: 12+ cores for video editing and 3D rendering
  • Socket Type: Make sure it matches your motherboard
